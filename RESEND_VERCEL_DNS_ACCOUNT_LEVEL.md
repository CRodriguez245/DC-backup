## üìç How to Access DNS Records in Vercel

### Step 1: Go to Team/Account Settings (Not Project Settings)

**You're currently in PROJECT settings. We need TEAM/ACCOUNT settings.**

1. **Look at the TOP of the page** (above the sidebar)
2. **Find your team/account name** (usually in top left or top navigation)
3. **Click on it** or look for a dropdown/selector
4. **Click "Settings"** or "Team Settings" (NOT project settings)

**OR:**

1. **Go to:** https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. **Click your team/account name** (top left or top navigation)
3. **Click "Settings"** from the dropdown
4. **Click "Domains"** in the left sidebar
5. **Find your domain** (`decisioncoach.io`)
6. **Click on it**
7. **Look for "DNS" tab or "DNS Records" section**

---

### Step 2: Alternative Path - Direct Domain Management

**Try this direct path:**

1. **Go to:** https://vercel.com/[your-team-name]/settings/domains
   (Replace `[your-team-name]` with your actual team name)

2. **OR:**
   - Click your profile/team icon (top right)
   - Look for "Team Settings" or "Account Settings"
   - Click "Domains"
   - Find `decisioncoach.io`
   - Click on it
   - Look for DNS section
